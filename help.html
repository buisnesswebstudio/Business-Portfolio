Help & Support

Need assistance? Here are answers to common questions. If you don’t find what you’re looking for, feel free to contact me anytime!

How do I request a website?

You can get in touch via the contact form or send me a direct message on Instagram at @business_web_studio1. I’ll reply as soon as possible to discuss your project.

What is the typical project timeline?

Small brochure-style websites generally take 2–4 weeks. More complex projects can take 6–12 weeks or longer depending on scope and requirements. We’ll agree on timelines during discovery.

Do you offer post-launch support?

Yes! I offer ongoing maintenance packages to keep your site secure and up-to-date. Reach out anytime to discuss options that fit your needs and budget.

How do you charge for projects?

I typically work on a project basis or with monthly retainers for ongoing support. Prices depend on scope and complexity. You’ll receive a detailed estimate before we start.

Can I update my website content myself?

Yes, if desired, I can build your site with a content management system (CMS) that lets you edit text and images easily without needing to code.

What technologies do you use?

I use modern web technologies focused on performance, accessibility, and clean design, including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript frameworks, and CMS integrations where appropriate.
